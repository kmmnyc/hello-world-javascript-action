# The “Blame America” Movement

The traditional values, moral assumptions, and social norms that hold society together being upended by the inevitable technological advances that come from progress — this is one of the biggest challenges currently faced by the West. Biggest, but completely expected; this conflict has confronted every culture to ever exist throughout history — it’s a natural component of civilization, a fact of life. 

It’s another thing entirely — unnatural, unexpected and unwelcome — when a segment of people set out to deliberately and willfully destablize, sabotage, and ultimately destroy their society. This is exactly what elite intellectuals have been attempting in America for decades, from the campus revolts of the 1960s to today’s Woke movement.
